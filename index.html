ABOUT

Hi, I’m Skhart Mercado, a frontend software engineer and freelance web developer based in Philippines. My journey started with a deep interest in programming and Robotics, and now I'm passionate about music, technology, and everything that makes the internet tick.

I’m currently studying at the University of the East, where I’m also involved in organizing events for the Automation, Robotics, and Mechatronics Society (ARMS) and Society Of Computer Engineering Students (SCpES). My work involves managing a mix of projects, freelance clients, and side projects, which keeps me constantly learning and adapting to the latest tools, libraries, and frameworks in many different platforms.

I began as a self-taught developer, focusing on C#, JavaScript and Java. Since then, I’ve transitioned to working primarily with Godot and Unity in Game Development. For simple mockup games, I’m a big fan of using Godot for its fast, developer-friendly, powerful game engine.

If you’re an enthusiast looking to start a web or app development project, a developer interested in collaborating on something cool, or just someone who wants to say hi, feel free to reach out. I’d love to work together!
